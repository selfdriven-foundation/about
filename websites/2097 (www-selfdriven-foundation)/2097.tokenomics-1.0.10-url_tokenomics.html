Tokens

Relating to tokenomics.

SDI (Identity)

Used to record identities on-chain, so can be referenced in other on-chain tokens (assets) e.g. a SDA.

SDA (Achievements)

Achievement tokens that are linked to known skills and the community member as represented by SDI tokens.

SDC (Community)

Used for recognition of community service, positive attribute, accountability etc.

SDF (Foundation)

Issued to foundation founding and advisory members and highly engaged community members.

About the selfdriven Community

Staking

In 2023, community members and supporters will be able to stake their tokens (ADA, SDC etc) to the selfdriven operated Cardano Stake Pool ("OctoPool"), supporting people learn and selfdrive their life.

As part of this process, we will also create guides to help learning communities set up their own stake pools (OctetPools) - which also adds to the decentralisation of the Cardano (blockchain) network!

Series 1

Launch November 2022

A limited series to get it started!

  • A series of 100 images that can be purchased by anyone that wishes to support the selfdriven Foundation @ $AU200 each, paid for as ADA.

  • Art is a digital image (delivered as an NFT on the Cardano blockchain), each image will have:
    • a unique ID
    • attributes based on characteristics of the images

  • Funds from purchasing of the art (NFT images) will be allocated as follows:
    • 2.5% to creating artist, in addition to allocation of SDF tokens.
    • 7.5% to the selfdriven Treasury.
    • 90% towards project funding as managed by the foundation.

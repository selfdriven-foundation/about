The images will be delivered as a NFTs using ipfs and the Cardano blockchain.

Each image will have a unique ID and be minted as a unique ARTi (artomics ID) token - allowing it to be traded.

Standard royalities fee based on trading is 5%.

Funds from purchasing and subsequent trading (and associated royality) of the Art (as ARTI NFTs) will be allocated as follows:

10% to creating artist.

10% to the selfdriven Treasury.

80% towards project funding as managed by the foundation.
